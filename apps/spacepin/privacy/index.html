Privacy Policy

SPACEPIN Last updated: March 22, 2026
1

Introduction

This app "SpacePin" (the "App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how it is used.

2

Information We Collect and Store

The App is a macOS app that displays notes and images in small pinned windows. The App does not require account registration and does not collect personal information, analytics data, advertising identifiers, or location information.

The App stores data locally on your device, including:

  • Note content and titles
  • Window position, size, and opacity
  • Note color and font size
  • Lock state, click-through state, and collapsed state
  • Other settings necessary to restore your notes and windows

The App only handles images that you choose to load. Loaded images are copied into the App's local storage area so they can be restored after the App restarts.

3

How We Use Information

The locally stored information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Display your notes and images in pinned windows
  • Restore your notes, images, and window state after restarting the App
  • Maintain your chosen appearance and behavior settings
4

Third-Party Services

The App does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party trackers.

The App does not upload your notes or images to our servers or to third-party servers.

5

Sharing with Third Parties

We do not provide your information, notes, or images to third parties unless required by law.

6

Data Retention and Deletion

Data saved by the App is stored locally on your device. It remains there until you delete it within the App or uninstall the App.

Images copied into the App's local storage for restoration are also stored locally and are not uploaded to any server.

7

Security

The App stores your notes, images, and related settings locally on your device. Because the App does not operate a backend server for this data, your content is not transmitted to us through normal use of the App.

8

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. The updated policy becomes effective when posted on this page.
